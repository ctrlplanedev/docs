<Warning>
  Self-hosting comes with significant complexity and operational overhead. For a
  simpler experience, we recommend using our fully-managed cloud platform at
  [https://app.ctrlplane.dev](https://app.ctrlplane.dev)
</Warning>

Ctrlplane can be deployed in a variety of ways. The most common way is to use
our Terraform module.
