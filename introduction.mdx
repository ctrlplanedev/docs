## What you can do with Ctrlplane

Ctrlplane streamlines and automates complex deployment processes across diverse infrastructures, helping teams scale operations efficiently and securely.

Here's what Ctrlplane offers:

| Capability               | Enables you to...                                                                                                                                       |
| :----------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Deployment Orchestration | Coordinate complex multi-environment deployments, enforce governance policies, and automate sophisticated rollout strategies across your infrastructure |
| Environment Management   | Create standardized environments with consistent configurations, manage environment-specific variables, and maintain deployment targets at scale        |
| Integration & Tools      | Connect seamlessly with existing CI/CD pipelines, major cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP), and container platforms like Kubernetes and Docker           |
| Release Management       | Track deployment status in real-time, implement version control policies, manage rollouts and rollbacks, and maintain deployment history                |
| Policy Controls          | Define and enforce deployment rules, implement role-based access control, maintain audit logs, and ensure compliance across teams                       |
| Automation               | Streamline repetitive deployment tasks, manage service dependencies, and scale deployments efficiently across multiple targets                          |

### Common Use Cases

- **Multi-Environment Deployments**: Coordinate releases across development,
  staging, and production environments with sophisticated scheduling and
  dependency management

- **Cloud & Container Operations**: Deploy and manage applications across
  multiple cloud providers and container orchestration platforms from a single
  control plane

- **Enterprise Governance**: Implement comprehensive access controls, maintain
  audit trails, and enforce organizational deployment policies

- **Infrastructure Scaling**: Automate deployment processes and efficiently
  manage resources as your infrastructure grows

- **Release Automation**: Streamline the entire release lifecycle from
  development to production with automated workflows and policy enforcement
